### Part 2: TCP Handshake Analysis
The connection attempt was analyzed using tcpdump. While egress SYN [S] packets were successfully transmitted from Foundations-Lab to secure.titancorp.com, no SYN-ACK [S.] was received.

Conclusion: 
Routing is successfully restored at the source. The timeout indicates a downstream block (Firewall DROP) or a missing return route on the target/gateway.
